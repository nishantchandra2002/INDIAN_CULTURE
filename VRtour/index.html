Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is renowned for its stunning beauty and is a symbol of eternal love.

Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal is a palace in the city of Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. Built from red and pink sandstone, it is on the edge of the City Palace, Jaipur, and extends to the Zenana, or women's chambers.

Elephant Caves

The 'City of Caves', on an island in the Sea of Oman close to Mumbai, contains a collection of rock art linked to the cult of Shiva. Here, Indian art has found one of its most perfect expressions, particularly the huge high reliefs in the main cave.

Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is known for its ancient temples and stunning ruins amidst boulder-strewn landscapes.
